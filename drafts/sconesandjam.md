## Ingredients Jam:
- Fruits (e.g. Raspberries, Blueberries)
- Chia seeds

### Optional:
- Agave syrup or honey
- Lemon 
- Vanilla Extract
- Almond Extract
- Spices


## Instructions: 
Heat the fruits in a pan until broken down. Add the chia seeds, syrup, lemon and extracts.
