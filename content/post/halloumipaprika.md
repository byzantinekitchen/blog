### Difficulty: very easy
### Time: 10 minutes


## Ingredients (6 people):
- Halloumi


### Seasoning:
- Paprika (more than one type if available)
- Thyme
- Pepper
- Cayenne pepper (optional)

## Instructions:
Cut the halloumi into small slices.
